# Upgrade Chess
Chess but you can level up your pieces and they have epic battles
<br>
Forget your openings, go ahead and play G3 or some other random move
<br>
For example, a pawn could survive a queen if it is leveled up enough
## In-Game Rules
Regular rules of chess apply unless the following things occur:
<br>
When there is a capture in regular chess, a fight is intiated in ChessRPG
- ### King Rules
Kings always win a fight initiated by itself(aka a capture)
<br>
Checkmate is still determined by regular chess rules
- ### Pawn Rules
When pawns promote, the promotion gives you a piece of the same level as the pawn
<br>
Pawns always win an en passant initiated by itself
